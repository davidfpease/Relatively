This button will collect the daily total of contract awards announced by the Department of Defense. The contract award amount is then compared to the average cost of building a new Middle School (~$26.5 million). While this comparison is imprecise at best, it still hopefully makes the scale of defense spending more tangible. DoD contract data is scraped from the most recent announcements located here.
